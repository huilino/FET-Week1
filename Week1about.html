Bonbonlicious is so much more than just a bakery shop. Our desserts are made fresh and of quality ingredients!

Bonbonlicious’s has been established for 5 years, located in a beautiful town of Fargo, ND. While cakes are at the heart of our kitchens, we’re big fans of other sweet treats as well: cookies, tart, brownies and macarons bring a little variety to the party.
